# Tatva.ai - Brand Identity

## Brand Name
**Tatva.ai**

### Meaning
- **Tatva** (तत्व) = Element/Essence/Truth in Sanskrit
- Represents the fundamental nature of sound and speech
- Connection to Indian philosophical tradition

## Tagline
**"Giving Voice to Ancient Wisdom"**

Alternative taglines:
- "Where Tradition Meets Technology"
- "Transforming Sound into Sacred Text"
- "Voice of the Ancients, Powered by AI"

## Logo Design

### Concept 1: Sanskrit "त" (Ta) Mark ⭐ RECOMMENDED

**Visual Elements:**
```
┌─────────────────┐
│    ╭─────╮      │
│    │  त  │      │ ← Devanagari "ta"
│    ╰─────╯      │   (first letter of Tatva)
│    Tatva.ai     │
└─────────────────┘
```

**Design Specs:**
- **Icon**: Devanagari letter "त" in a modern geometric style
- **Shape**: Rounded square or circle background
- **Gradient**: Orange (#F97316) → Red (#DC2626) → Purple (#7C3AED)
- **Font**: Inter (modern) or Noto Sans (for Indian script feel)

**Colors:**
- Primary: Orange-500 (#F97316) - Energy, warmth, India
- Secondary: Purple-600 (#7C3AED) - Wisdom, spirituality, AI
- Accent: Red-500 (#EF4444) - Passion, transformation
- Background: White or Slate-50 (#F8FAFC)
- Text: Slate-900 (#0F172A)

### Logo Variations

**Primary Logo:**
- Full color with gradient
- For web, presentations, marketing

**Monochrome:**
- Single color (orange or purple)
- For print, watermarks, favicon

**Icon Only:**
- Just the "त" symbol
- For app icon, social media avatar
- Size: 512x512px, 192x192px, 64x64px
